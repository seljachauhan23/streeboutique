1. What is Cash on Delivery?

Cash on Delivery is one of the payment methods for making purchases on Amazon.in. When you select Cash on Delivery as your payment method, you don't have to make any advance payment. You pay for your order only when you receive it. Click here to know more about Cash on Delivery.

2. How can I order the same items again?

Follow the steps below to order an item again -
1. Go to Your Orders.
2. Click on the Buy Again Tab. You will see a list of items you had previously purchased. Add to Cart the items you wish to purchase again.
3. Go to the cart and follow the onscreen instruction to complete the order.

3. What about the quality of the products?

At streeboutique, we have stringent quality checks and ensure that we onboard only high-quality suppliers. In case, the quality is not up to the mark, we provide easy returns.
Further, we actively take user feedback on product quality, post delivery.

4. How do I check the current status of my order?

You can review the status of your orders and other related tracking information by visiting the "My Account" section. You can instantly see the status of a specific order and all your past orders.

5. Why is my Order not showing?

It generally takes 2-3 weeks for the product to be delivered from the order date.
You can track your order in the "My Orders" Section. If it is still not showing, please drop an email to customer service at streeboutique20@gmail.com.

6. What if I have forgotten my account password?

While logging in to your account, you may use OTP option that will allow you to access your account. Alternatively, you may contact streeboutique support team to reset your account password.

7. How do I add items to my wishlist?

Go to 'My Wishlist' menu.
Click on the 'Heart' to deselect it and remove the product from your wishlist.

8. How do I remove or delete items from my wishlist?

Go to 'My Wishlist' menu.
Click on the 'Heart' to deselect it and remove the product from your wishlist.

9. How do I cancel an order?

If you wish to cancel an order that has been shipped but has not yet been delivered, kindly refuse the shipment at the time of delivery.

10. How can I place International orders?

Our products are available in only India. We are not sell our product in other countries.
